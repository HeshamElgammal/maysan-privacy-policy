## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account information:** Name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account.
- **Profile information:** Resume/CV, work experience, education, skills, profile photo, and other details you add to your profile (for job seekers) or company details (for employers).
- **Job applications and listings:** Content of applications, job postings, messages, and communications you send through the App.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **Usage data:** How you use the App (e.g., features used, time spent), crash reports, and performance data to improve the App.
- **Location data:** Only if you choose to enable location services (e.g., for job search by location). You can disable this in your device settings.

### 1.3 Information From Third Parties

- If you sign in with a third-party service (e.g., Google, Apple), we may receive basic profile information that you have allowed that service to share with us.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Maysan service.
- Create and manage your account and profile.
- Match job seekers with opportunities and employers with candidates.
- Send you notifications about jobs, applications, and relevant updates (you can manage preferences in the App).
- Respond to your requests, comments, and support inquiries.
- Analyze usage to improve our App and user experience.
- Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and the rights of others.
- Send important service-related or security notices.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information only in the following situations:

- **With other users as needed for the service:** For example, your profile and application details may be visible to employers when you apply, or to job seekers when you post jobs.
- **Service providers:** With trusted partners who help us operate the App (e.g., hosting, analytics, customer support), under strict confidentiality and data protection agreements.
- **Legal requirements:** When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
- **Business transfers:** In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition of our business, subject to the same privacy commitments.

We do not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing.
